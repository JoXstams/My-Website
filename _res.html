Project Idea


Overview

“MyP.A.” – an Artificial Intelligence Assistant (AIA). Just say something into a microphone and the app will record what you say and create your entry. Your own Personal Assistant that keeps your calendar and schedule and keeps reminders. A shopping/wish list and meal planner that can connect to an online search via browser or installed app. A section for diary entries and notes. Integrated alarm. A budget manager which can connect to installed apps and payment services. Link to Social media accounts. Sync to products like Google nest and Amazon Alexa and make notes or entries by speaking to your home hub.


Motivation

Among the millions of apps available today, Productivity apps are seventh amongst the highest downloaded apps on Google Play Store, with a penetration rate of 91.67% for productivity. In the description of an article of The Financial Express, 2016-09-27; it is quoted that David Willis, suggested that emergence of virtual personal assistants and bots would replace some of the functions performed by apps and the importance of apps delivering services will diminish. With evolving technology and peoples’ demand to have everything they need available in the easiest means, Amazon made this even more achievable in April 2017, when they released a service for building conventional interfaces for all types of virtual assistant or interface.


Description

The Calendar would keep track of important dates personalised to you, with ability to sync to your other calendars such as Samsung calendar, Google calendar, and other calendar services you might use. The Schedule would give you ability to plan by day, week or month at hourly intervals and would also sync with calendar and all email accounts. Ask your PA to create and manage your reminders and personalize tones and display options and alternative option to send e-mail reminders. Let your ‘MyPA’ know of your appointments, and it will log it everywhere it needs to be, including an email to confirm it has been booked in your schedule. All you need to do is open app and press microphone or use sidebar access and tell your MyPA what you need it to do for you.
A Shopping list and wish list section, you might have a grocery list saved of items you get regularly and maybe another that changes each week depending on the meals you choose in the integrated meal planner. making it easy to order one or more lists online, or simply have the list(s) easy to access when you’re in the shop. A ‘wish’ list section, where you can search, save and purchase items you may need or be interested in. Both Shopping and wish list as well as integrated meal planner have access to a search which will give you ‘shopping’ search results from you default browser or redirect to shopping apps installed on your phone. A Diary and Notes section, where those who just like to write can enter in the diary entry and if you just need to jot some notes you can do that in the note pad, use your keyboard, voice-to-text or simply say “diary entry” or “note” to your MyPA and when page loads, type or press microphone at top to speak.
A budget planner and manager, enter information manually or connect to apps or websites of any utility services, rental payment, subscription services and any other service or payments that have app or website payment options. Your MyPA will calculate your budget at your chosen intermittence (weekly, fortnightly, monthly). Sync the budget section to your emails and calendar and arrange for your ‘MyPA’ to set up and complete manual payments on required dates. The budget section will also provide links to some budgeting assistance.
Sync your MyPA with you home hub eg. Amazon Alexa or Google Nest and talk to your MyPA through your home hub. Link to social media accounts.
Give your MyPA a name and face, with AI integration, it will become your best friend.
Permissions to access and sync to external accounts and services like voice-to-text would need to be clarified. Access to a pop-up or sidebar access for quick access, potentially a shortcut or widget from home screen too. Application developers and cloud developers alongside a machine learning architect would need to be involved in entire process of these aspects. Would need to be able to work on Android and Apple devices, consider even Microsoft integration.


Tools & Technology

Essential technologies for creating an AI personal assistant are; Cloud solution technology, Artificial Intelligence and Internet of things (IoT) technology for more advanced.
Python programming language is best used for the development of AI personal assistants, alternate programming languages to use are; Lisp, Java and Prolog. However, Python being the most renowned.
The Modules essential to install for use in python are; Pyttsx3, SpeechRecognition, Webbrowser, Wikipedia, Ecapture, Datetime, Os, Time, Subprocess, Json, Wolfram alpha.
Methods required to be used are; Speak, Take Query, Hello, takeCommand, Main.
Tools such as NumPy, Matplotlib, Pandas, Scikit, Theano, AIMA, EasyAi, PyBrain amongst others will make an AIA creation much easier.


Skill Requirements

Level of skill required for development of an AIA creation varies depending on the degree of complication of the project itself.
Simple creations could require just knowledge of python language and using the tools suggested, today there are many code templates which allow even novice programmers to create an AIA.
However, for more complicated projects, such as my idea, the programmer may need further skills such as cloud computing experience, machine learning knowledge and multi-language programming skills.
Programming and other IT skills have become widely and easily available to learn, with many learning platforms encompassing multitudes of choices from free basic concepts tutorials to formal learning.


Outcome

“MyPA” on completion would provide customers with a virtual personal assistant that can be used for personal, work and business purposes.
The concept of AIA’s is becoming more prominent in how businesses operate, and this will soon be a thing in personal everyday lives.
With AIA technologies, the time where we can use an assistant to keep us organised is on approach, without big diaries and notebooks or even sticky notes. Also not have to hire a personal assistant would save money for many.
MyPA will make organizing your life simpler than ever before.
